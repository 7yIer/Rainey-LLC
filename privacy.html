Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and other details you provide voluntarily.
  • Usage Data: Device type, operating system, app version, usage patterns, and access timestamps.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve and personalize user experience
  • To communicate updates and changes
  • To provide customer support
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To send promotional content (with consent)

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Face Data Collection and Usage

  • Facial images and scan data may be collected for analysis features.
  • Face data is used only for providing analysis functionality and is primarily stored locally on your device.
  • Face data may be temporarily transmitted to OpenAI/ChatGPT for analysis purposes only and is not permanently stored on our servers.
  • We do not share face data with third parties.
  • Data stored on your device remains until you uninstall the app.

5. Consent to Share Consumption Data with Apple

By using our app and making in-app purchases, you consent to our sharing of data regarding your usage and consumption of purchased content with Apple, as part of our efforts to resolve refund requests.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Please review this policy regularly.
